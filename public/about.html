We are passionate University of Amsterdam students revolutionizing investment strategies through cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies.

Our Story

Traidant was born from a shared vision among five ambitious University of Amsterdam students who recognized the transformative potential of artificial intelligence in financial markets.

As part of our "Project: Entrepreneurship" course, we embarked on a journey to create something that could genuinely impact how people approach investing. What started as an academic project quickly evolved into a comprehensive AI-powered investment platform.

Our diverse backgrounds in computer science, economics, and data science converged to create a unique perspective on financial technology. We saw an opportunity to democratize sophisticated investment strategies that were previously only available to institutional investors.

Based in Amsterdam

Located at the heart of Europe's financial and technology hub, the University of Amsterdam provides us with access to world-class resources, mentorship, and a vibrant ecosystem of innovation and entrepreneurship.

Amsterdam's position as a leading fintech center, combined with the Netherlands' progressive approach to financial regulation and technology, creates the perfect environment for developing cutting-edge AI investment solutions.
